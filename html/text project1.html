INTRODUCTION

Probably one of the best cars to exist with a 5.0 litre twin turbo V8 engine producing 1280 hp on standard gasoline with advanced aerodynamic offer up to 1400 kg of downforce, making it the ultimate track weapon.

ENGINE LAYOUT

The Koenigsegg Light Speed Transmission (LST) represents a scale of evolution not seen since the development of modern dual-clutch technology at the turn of the century. LST consists of nine forward gears and seven wet, multi-disc clutches in one compact, ultra-light package. The LST is capable of upward and downward gear changes at near light speed thanks to the simultaneous opening and closing of clutches that allows for totally seamless acceleration and deceleration. That LST gear changes can occur in virtually zero time between adjacent gears is impressive. What takes LST from impressive to evolutionary, however, is that these shift-time benefits apply when changing between any forward gears.

INTERIOR LAYOUT

The Koenigsegg Jesko interior combines minimalist design with cutting-edge technology and luxury materials. It features a driver-focused cockpit with a compact digital SmartCluster display mounted directly on the steering wheel, which remains visible even as the wheel turns. The cabin is trimmed with premium materials like leather, Alcantara, and carbon fiber, reflecting the car's high-performance ethos. Despite its race-ready nature, the Jesko offers amenities like climate control, infotainment with Apple CarPlay, and comfortable bucket seats, blending performance with refinement.
