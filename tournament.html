Special Events

The arcade is hosting an exciting 90s retro gaming tournament special that’s sure to bring out the inner child in everyone. Weather your a teen, nostalgic adult, or just family. Players will face off in classic games like Street Fighter II and Pac-Man, with the grand prize being a fully restored vintage arcade machine.

Origin
Designed for ages 12 and up, this event brings the classic gaming experience to life with a vibrant and nostalgic atmosphere.
Material
The tournament will feature top-of-the-line arcade machines, ensuring smooth gameplay and an authentic 90s feel.
Dimensions
The event will take place in the main arcade hall, which is designed to accommodate a large crowd and ensure comfortable participation for all players.
Finish
Expect high-energy competition, immersive lighting, and the sounds of the most beloved retro games as they light up the arcade.
Includes
Admission to the tournament, access to all participating games, and a chance to win amazing prizes including the grand vintage arcade machine!
Considerations
Open to all skill levels. Whether you're a seasoned player or just there for the fun, it’s an event everyone can enjoy!
